### **Data Engineering & Systems**

- [*Fundamentals of Data Engineering* by Joe Reis and Matt Housley (2022)](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/fundamentals-of-data/9781098108308/): Published by O'Reilly Media.
- [*The Data Warehouse Toolkit, 3rd Ed.* by Ralph Kimball (2013)](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/The+Data+Warehouse+Toolkit%3A+The+Definitive+Guide+to+Dimensional+Modeling%2C+3rd+Edition-p-9781118530801): Published by Wiley.
- [*Analytics Engineering with SQL and dbt* by Rui Machado and Hélder Russa (2023)](https://www.packtpub.com/product/analytics-engineering-with-sql-and-dbt/9781803241129): Published by Packt.
- [*Streaming Systems* by Tyler Akidau, Slava Chernyak, and Reuven Lax (2018)](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/streaming-systems/9781491983867/): Published by O'Reilly Media.
- [*Readings in Database Systems (The "Red Book"), 5th Ed.* edited by Bailis, Hellerstein, and Stonebraker (2015)](http://www.redbook.io/): Officially available for free online at this URL.
- [*Elements of Information Theory, 2nd Ed.* by Cover and Thomas](https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Elements+of+Information+Theory%2C+2nd+Edition-p-9780471241959): Published by Wiley.
- [*Differential Equations, Dynamical Systems, and an Introduction to Chaos, 3rd Ed.* by Hirsch, Smale, and Devaney](https://www.elsevier.com/books/differential-equations-dynamical-systems-and-an-introduction-to-chaos/hirsch/978-0-12-382010-5): Published by Academic Press (an imprint of Elsevier).
